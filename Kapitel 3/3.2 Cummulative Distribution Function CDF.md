Expectations of different distributions:
$Bernoulli(p) = p$
$Geometric(p) = \frac{1}{p}$
$Poisson(\lambda) = \lambda$
$Binomial(n,p) = np$
$Pascal(m,p) = \frac{m}{p}$
Varians of different distributions:
$Bernoulli(p) = p(1-p)$
$Geometric(p) = \frac{1-p}{p^{2}}$
$Poisson(\lambda) = \lambda$
$Binomial(n,p) = np(1-p)$
$Pascal(m,p) = m\frac{1-p}{p^{2}}$
